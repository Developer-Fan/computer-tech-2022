Basil Pesto Chicken(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 120g fettuccine
  • 1 ½ tb basil pesto
  • 1 rasher bacon
  • ¾ cup cream
  • ½ tb fresh basil
  • 120g chicken breast
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 tb parmesan cheese

Method

  1. Place hot water in a pot, place a lid on it and put it on the stovetop on the highest temperature.
  2. When the water has boiled, remove the lid and add the fettuccine.
  3. Cook for approximately 10mins or until al dente.
  4. In the meantime, using the red chopping board, remove the fat from the bacon and dice.
  5. Thinly slice the chicken.
  6. With a separate board and knife, chop the fresh basil.
  7. Place a frying pan on a medium heat, add spray oil and cook bacon for approximately 1 minute.
  8. Add the chicken and cook for approx 5 mins or until the chicken is lightly browned and cooked all the way through.
  9. Add the pesto, cream and a pinch of salt and pepper to the pan.
  10. Reduce heat to medium/low and cook for approx 5mins or until the creamy sauce has thickened.
  11. Carefully drain the pasta once it is cooked and separate into two bowls.
  12. Top with the chicken sauce mixture.
  13. Garnish with fresh basil and parmesan cheese.
