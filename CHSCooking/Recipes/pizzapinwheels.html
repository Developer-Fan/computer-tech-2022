Pizza Pinwheels (serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
  • 2 tb tomato paste
  • 2 tb cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tb parmesan cheese
  • 1 tb shredded cheese
  • 1 rasher bacon
  • ½ tb milk
  • ½ ring pineapple (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees celsius. Line a flat baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Soften cream cheese (if needed), mix together with the tomato paste.
  3. Place the bacon on a red chopping board. Remove rind and excess fat from the bacon, dice the rest.
  4. Cut pineapple into smaller pieces.
  5. Spread the cream cheese/tomato paste mixture evenly over the pastry sheet.
  6. Sprinkle over the diced bacon, both cheeses and the pineapple.
  7. Roll up tightly and then slice into 1 cm pieces. Lay flat on the baking tray.
  8. Lightly brush the tops with milk, using a pastry brush.
  9. Bake in the oven for approx 15 mins or until pastry is puffed and lightly golden.
