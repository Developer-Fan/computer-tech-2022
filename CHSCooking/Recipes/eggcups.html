Scrambled Egg Toast Cups(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 6 slices wholegrain bread
  • 15g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp chives
  • ½ tomato
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsely

Method

  1. Collect equipment and ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  3. Cut crusts off bread and soften butter.
  4. Spread butter evenly over the 6 slices.
  5. Place each slice, buttered side down into a large muffin tray.
  6. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
  7. Put spray oil into a large frying pan and cook egg on a medium heat until scrambled.
  8. Slice tomato and chop chives.
  9. Fill bread cases with scrambled egg.
  10. Top with tomato and cheese, garnish with parsley.
  11. Bake in oven for 5-10mins or until cheese hasmelted.
  12. Remove cups from oven with two oven mitts.
  13. Allow to cool slightly then remove and garnish with parsley.
