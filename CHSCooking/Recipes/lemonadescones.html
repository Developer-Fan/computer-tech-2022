Lemonade Scones(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-raising flour, sifted
  • 1/8 cup caster sugar
  • 1/4 cup thickened cream
  • 1/4 cup lemonade
  • a pinch of salt
  • ½ tb milk
  • extra flour (to knead on bench)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C. Line a flat baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Sift the 1 cup of flour into a large bowl, add sugar and salt.
  3. Add the cream and lemonade and mix with a butter knife to form a soft dough.
  4. Place the dough onto a clean and lightly floured workbench. Knead gently until combined.
  5. Press the dough with your hands to a thickness of approx 2cm.
  6. Use a cookie cutter of your choice, to cut scones. Keep re-rolling the dough if needed, so you can use up as much dough as possible.
  7. Place scones close together on the baking tray.
  8. Lightly brush the tops with milk using a pastry brush.
  9. Bake for approx 10-15 minutes or until very lightly browned and cooked in the centre.
  10. Serve with toppings of your choice - for example, jam, butter or whipped cream.
