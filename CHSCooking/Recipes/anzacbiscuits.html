Anzac Biscuits(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons golden syrup
  • ½ teaspoon bicarb soda
  • ½ cup coconut
  • 125g margarine
  • 1 tablespoon boiling water

Method

  1. Method Preheat oven to 180◦C.
  2. Place oats, sugar and coconut into a large bowl, sift in the plain flour.
  3. In a separate bowl, melt margarine and golden syrup in the microwave.
  4. Combine the boiling water and bicarb soda, mix well.
  5. Add to margarine and mix well.
  6. Add those ingredients to the flour mixture.
  7. Combine well Place a sheet of baking paper on a baking tray.
  8. Roll small balls of mixture and space out on the baking tray as they will expand.
  9. It should make approx 9-10 biscuits.
  10. Press the tops with the back of a fork to flatten slightly.
  11. Bake for approximately 15 mins or until lightly golden.
  12. Allow to cool on trays before removing.
