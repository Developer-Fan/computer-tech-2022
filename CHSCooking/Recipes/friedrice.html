Fried Rice(serves 2)

Ingredients

  • 1 whole egg
  • ½ carrot
  • 1 tb frozen peas
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • ¼ stick celery
  • 1 tb frozen corn
  • 1 rasher bacon
  • 1 tb soy sauce
  • ¼ brown onion
  • spray oil
  • sweet chilli sauce (optional)

Method

  1. Collect ingredients and equipment.
  2. Place the bacon on a red chopping board. Remove the excess fat with a sharp knife and dice the remaining amount.
  3. On the green or wooden chopping board, dice the onion, carrot and celery.
  4. Place the frying pan on a medium heat and spray with spray oil.
  5. Whisk the egg in a measuring jug with a fork and pour into frying pan. Cook one one side, flip and cook the other side. Remove egg from pan and set aside. Dice or cut into slices once cooled.
  6. Add a little more spray oil to frying pan (if needed) and cook the onion and bacon until lightly golden.
  7. Add the diced carrot, celery, peas and corn, cook for a few mins to soften.
  8. Add the rice and soy sauce and stir through well.
  9. Stir the egg through the fried rice.
  10. (optional) Serve rice in bowls with any remaining egg, and sweet chilli sauce.
